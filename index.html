A Little About Me...

My name is Travis Arnold. I am currently a student studying CS and Design at Eastern Washington University. I have been working my way towards graduating for a little over five years now and am almost done. I currently work as a mechanic for my parents business, Bicycle Butler. Whether at work or not I am always looking for a way to learn something new because I believe every skill makes you a much more well rounded and unique person. I look at myself and see a mechanic, a salesperson, a student, a programmer, a designer, an individual.
